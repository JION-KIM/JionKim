Moon

Do you like to see the moon? I like it very much. In addition, I like to take pictures of the moon. When taking a walk at night, there are days when the moon looks exceptionally clear and bright. On such a day, I think I was lucky and I feel happy about that day anyway. So, the gallery on my cell phone is full of pictures taken every time I look at the moon. So I want to introduce you to some of the moon pictures I took.



This picture is the moon we can see normally.
While walking in a park near my house, I sat down for a while and took a picture with my cell phone.


This is the first photo of the moon that emits red light.
And the size of the moon was very large.
I took it on my way home because it was so amazing, but this picture reminds me of IU's strawberry moon song.


Isn't this picture really cool? This month's picture was taken by me putting my cell phone on an astronomical telescope. As a good opportunity, I could see the moon through the astronomical telescope, and it was very nice that day because the weather was nice.
